Clearing Past Floats

There are cases where you may not want an element to wrap next to a floated element. In these cases you will want to clear the element past the floated element. The clear property by default has the value of none, but can also have the values of left, right, and both to clear an element past a floated left, floated right or both left and right floated elements respectively. Things to try:

  • Remove the float declaration on the img tag to show that the images are contained when not floated.
